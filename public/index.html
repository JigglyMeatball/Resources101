AfterImpact accident aftercare guide

Had an accident? Know what not to miss next.

A plain-language resource to help people understand what to watch for, what to ask, what to document, and how to move forward if life changes after an accident.

Start with the first 72 hours Check warning signs

Emergency note

If someone has trouble breathing, chest pain, heavy bleeding, loss of consciousness, confusion, seizure, severe head/neck/back pain, new weakness, repeated vomiting, or severe/worsening symptoms, seek emergency help now.

What this site helps with

Immediate aftermath

Get safe, know when to seek urgent help, write down what happened, and avoid missing early follow-up steps.

Medical visit preparation

Use scripts and question lists so you can explain the accident clearly and ask what to watch for next.

Long-term recovery

Track symptoms, function, work changes, family impact, support needs, and life adjustments if recovery is not quick.

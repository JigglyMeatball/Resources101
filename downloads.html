Tools

Printable checklists and trackers

These are starter tools to help people organize what happened, what changed, what was said, and what comes next.

Starter tools

First 72 Hours Checklist

What to do first, what to ask, and what to save after an accident.

Symptom Tracker

Track pain, headache, dizziness, sleep, mood, swelling, weakness, and activity limits.

Medical Visit Question Sheet

Questions to bring to urgent care, ER, primary care, or follow-up visits.

Accident Documentation Log

Record dates, photos, witnesses, reports, receipts, calls, and claim numbers.

Insurance Call Log

Track who you called, reference numbers, what they said, next steps, and deadlines.

Long-Term Recovery Tracker

Track function, work changes, home changes, support needs, and new routines.
